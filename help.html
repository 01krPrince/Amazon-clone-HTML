Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?
To track your order, go to Your Orders in your account. You'll be able to see the delivery status of all your recent orders. Click on "Track package" to see detailed tracking information.
How do I return an item?
To return an item, go to Your Orders and select the item you wish to return. Click "Return or replace items" and follow the on-screen instructions. You'll be able to print a return label if eligible for free returns.
What are the benefits of Amazon Prime?
Amazon Prime benefits include FREE fast delivery, streaming of movies, TV shows and music, exclusive shopping deals and selection, and more. To learn more, visit the Amazon Prime page.
How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the Sign-In page and click on "Forgot your password?". Enter the email address or mobile phone number associated with your account and follow the instructions sent to you.
When will I receive my refund?
Once we receive your return, it takes 2-3 business days to process the refund. After processing, it may take 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment method.
